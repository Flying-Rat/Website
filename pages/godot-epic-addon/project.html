Rat-ical Racers

Get ready for a high-flying, wheel-spinning adventure with Rat-ical Racers! Choose your rodent, customize your contraption, and race through absurd tracks filled with power-ups and perilous obstacles. Can you become the ultimate "Flying Rat" champion?

Welcome to the chaotic and charming world of Rat-ical Racers, the brand new kart-racing sensation from Flying Rat Studio! Prepare for an adrenaline-pumping, laugh-out-loud experience where tiny rodents with huge ambitions take to the tracks in their wildest, most wonderful contraptions.

Game Features

Choose Your Rat-lete

Select from a quirky roster of unique rat characters, each with their own special stats and adorable animations.

Crazy Contraptions

Build and customize your perfect racing machine! Mix and match wheels, wings, engines, and even bathtubs to create a vehicle that's uniquely yours. Will you go for speed, defense, or aerial acrobatics?

Wild & Whacky Tracks

Race across a variety of imaginative environments, from bustling cityscapes made of pipes and vents to lush, overgrown gardens with giant obstacles. Each track is designed for maximum fun and unexpected twists!

Power-Up Pandemonium

Unleash a hilarious arsenal of power-ups to gain an advantage or wreak havoc on your opponents. From super-speed cheese boosts to sticky traps and aerial attacks, anything goes!

Multiplayer Mayhem

Challenge your friends in local split-screen multiplayer or race against other "Flying Rats" online to prove who's the fastest rodent on the block.

Championship Climb

Progress through multiple racing championships, unlock new characters and customization options, and strive to earn the coveted "Flying Rat" trophy!

Join the race, embrace the chaos, and discover why life in the fast lane is so much better when you're a Rat-ical Racer!
